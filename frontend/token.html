About

What is BuiltByLuca?

BuiltByLuca (ticker 0xLuke) is a community-focused token created by an independent builder obsessed with automation, bots and privacy-friendly tools. It is designed to experiment with new products, reward early supporters and unlock access to the BuiltByLuca ecosystem.

The token runs on the Solana network and is initially launched via Pump.fun, making it easy for anyone to join directly from a browser or mobile wallet.
